## Statement of capital (prescribed particulars of rights attached to Please give the prescribed particulars of rights attached to shares for each class of share shown in the share capital tables in Section 4. Class of share Ordinary Prescribed particulars - One vote per share. - Not redeemable. - Each share carries the right to participate in a distribution. - Each share carries the right on a return of assets on liquidation or capital reduction to participate after paying the holders of the preference shares £1 per share, in the next £2,898,344 and each share will also participate in any surplus ranking pari passu with the A ordinary shares. Class of share Preference Prescribed particulars - Non-voting. Shares are redeemable. - Each share does not carry a right to participate in a distribution. - Each share carries the right to participate on a return of assets on liquidation or capital reduction first by receiving £1 per preference share. Class of share A ordinary Prescribed particulars One vote per share. Not redeemable. Each share carries the right to participate in a distribution.

 Prescribed particulars of rights attached to shares

The particulars are:

- a particulars of any voting rights, including rights that arise only in certain circumstances;
- particulars of any rights, as respects dividends, to participate in a distribution:
- particulars of any rights, as respects capital, to participate in a distribution (including on winding up); and
- whether the shares are to be redeemed or are liable to be redeemed at the option of the company or the shareholder.

A separate table must be used for each class of share.
